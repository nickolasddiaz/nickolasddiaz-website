For Pecola, “her desire for the bluest eyes is because of Pecola's dissatisfaction for who she is, with her world, and of her want for something greater,” (de Weever). Another reason she wanted blue eyes is that in her mind she thought that the bluest eye is the most beautiful thing in the world, and it would solve all her suffering and problems.

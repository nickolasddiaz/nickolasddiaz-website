On January 1 they created a new camp called the Patience Camp. On April 6 the ice pack broke in half and the crew had to get on boats. On April 23 the crew landed on Elephant Island. However on the next day, the three men including Shackleton set sail to South Georgia to get rescued. However the other twenty-three people were stuck on Elephant Island. They couldn’t bring all of them because the all boats got destroyed but one boat survived and they could only ﬁt three on. On May 10 the three people reached South Georgia Island. On the island, Shackleton went to Thoralf Sórlle who was the station foreman and got rescued along with the two other men.

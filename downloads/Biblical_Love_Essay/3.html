Diaz 3 There are many rules to Agape love, in order to have a loving relationship with God. The first one is that God’s love is perfect and he loves everyone. It doesn't matter what bad things people have done, nothing in the world good or bad will change God’s love. “God gives this love without condition, unreservedly to those who are undeserving and inferior to himself” (crosswalk.com). The second is that God is always going to want the best. Just like a parent he will put people through hard trials or harsh punishments in order to make people grow and mature. If God did not do this then everyone would be very evil and do everything they wanted to do and cause chaos. “Those people will be shocked to find themselves spending eternity in hell if they believe a loving God would not condemn anyone to hell.” (bible.org). It is a harsh reality but keep in mind that God is alway suffering with us. The third reason is that God's love is personal. “God’s love is an exercise of His goodness towards individual sinners” (bible.org). Having a relationship with God is personal. It is called prayer and it is very important to pray everyday and talk about anything. God’s love doesn’t just extend to talking, he can empower us to do more than we can do by ourselves. With the holy spirit we can do miracles, remove demons and compel us to fight our evil temptations. The last reason is that we have to make a great attempt to stop sinning. “Whoever has my commands and keeps them is the one who loves me. The one who loves me will be loved by my Father, and I too will love them and show myself to them” (John 14:21). The reason why it is important is that the more we sin the farther we get from God. Reducing the amount of sin shows God that we are growing and trying to be more like him. It is also important to show others God’s love to us just like Jesus did on earth. “My command is this: Love each other as I have loved you.
